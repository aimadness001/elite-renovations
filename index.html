Meet the Owner

Marco D'Angelo

Marco founded Beyond Rhare Construction & Renovation over 12 years ago with a simple belief: renovations should be stress-free and the results should last a lifetime. Born and raised in Toronto, Marco trained as a carpenter before building Beyond Rhare Construction & Renovation into one of the GTA's most trusted kitchen and bathroom renovation studios.

Today, Marco personally oversees every project's design phase, ensuring each home gets the same hands-on attention his very first client received — from a downtown condo refresh to full custom builds in Forest Hill.
