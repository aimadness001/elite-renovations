Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your renovation with us.

How long does a typical renovation take?+

Most kitchen renovations take 4–8 weeks and bathrooms 2–4 weeks, depending on scope. Full home remodels can run 3–6 months. We'll give you a detailed timeline before work begins.

Do I need permits for my renovation?+

Many renovations involving plumbing, electrical, or structural changes require permits. We handle all permitting and inspections with the City of Toronto on your behalf.

How much dust and mess should I expect?+

We use containment barriers, negative-air filtration, and daily site cleanup to keep dust and debris to a minimum throughout your home.

Do you offer fixed-price quotes?+

Yes. After our design phase, you'll receive a detailed, fixed-price quote with no hidden fees or surprise change orders.

What areas do you serve?+

We proudly serve Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area, including Etobicoke, North York, Mississauga, and Leslieville.

Do you offer warranties on your work?+

Every project is backed by our comprehensive 5-year workmanship warranty, in addition to manufacturer warranties on materials and fixtures.
